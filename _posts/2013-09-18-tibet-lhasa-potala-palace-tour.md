Tibet Lhasa Potala Palace tour is trilling and exciting tour package for those who want to visit all the interesting place of Tibet in short period of time.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">We organizes 7 night 8 days Tibet Lhasa Potala Palace tour which starts from Kathmandu. The package includes the visit of some important beautiful places of Tibet including the Potala Palace, Norbulinka Palace, Drepung Monastery, Sera Monastery, Ganden Monastery, Gyante, Jokhang Temple, Barkher Market and many places around the city.</p>
