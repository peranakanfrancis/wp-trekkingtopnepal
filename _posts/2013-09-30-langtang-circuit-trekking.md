<p style="text-align: justify;"><strong>Langtang trekking region</strong> is well known for the their wildlife which includes the most notable animals such as the red pandas, Himalayan black bear, wild boar and white monkeys. <strong>Langtang circuit trek</strong> is design for those trekkers who want to explore and go wild with the nature. Langtang circuit trekking is longer trekking in Langtang region and brings lots of adventurous. Langtang circuit trekking passes through the some of the important places of Langtang region which includes the <strong>Langtang Valley</strong>, the Holy Lake <strong>Gosaikunda</strong> and the adventure pass <strong>Lauri Binayak</strong> (4,610 m). Langtang circuit trekking also enhanced with the amazing views of the snow-capped mountains such as Langtang Lirung, Pemthang Ri, Langshisha Ri, Dorje Lakpa, and Kangchenpo, Tilman’s fluted peak seem really awesome.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">As other trekking, Lantang circuit trekking is also start from the Syabru Bensi and finally ends to the Sundarijal. The trekking trails of Langtang circuit follows through larch and rhododendron forests which is rich in wild flowers and birds, explore the head of the valley getting close to the glacier system.</p>
